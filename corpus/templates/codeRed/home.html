ABOUT US

The CompSoc IEEE NITK Student Branch is a vibrant community of students passionate about Computer Science. We aim to foster a culture of learning and exploration of diverse technologies such as Algorithms, Systems, Machine Learning, Development, Web3, and many more. Through peer discussions and a variety of events, including Knowledge Sharing Sessions, CTFs, and Hackathons, we strive to inspire curiosity and collaboration among our members.
