About the Event

Entwine: Forged to Fly is a technical event that challenges students to design using CAD tools and analyze using CFD software. It encourages creative, interdisciplinary thinking in engineering design and aerodynamics.

Event Details

When

Who

  • Max 3 per team

How

Register and team up on Unstop

Challenge Format

  • Section 1: Ideation & Design (CAD Modeling)
    Participants must design a CAD model for the problem statement provided under the constraints specified. The team is free to choose any CAD software (Fusion 360, Onshape, SolidWorks, etc.).
  • Section 2: Simulation & Aerodynamic Evaluation (CFD in ANSYS Fluent)
    Once the design is finalized, teams must analyze the aerodynamic surface using CFD simulations in ANSYS Fluent.
